Seville Road Library

Welcome to our community library. Discover, learn, and grow.

Extensive Book Collection

Seville Road Library offers a diverse selection of books for all ages, including fiction, non-fiction, graphic novels, and large print editions. Whether you're after a classic or the latest bestseller, you'll find something that suits your reading taste in a quiet, welcoming atmosphere designed for discovery and reflection.

Free Public Wi-Fi

Stay connected with free public Wi-Fi and access to library computers. Browse the internet, complete assignments, or use printing and photocopying services. The library provides accessible digital resources and is a great spot for working remotely or catching up on emails in a calm and professional environment.

Family Programs

The library hosts regular programs for young readers including First 5 Forever storytime sessions and school holiday activities. These events encourage early literacy, creativity, and social engagement, making the library a valuable space for families and caregivers to enjoy meaningful learning experiences together.

Quiet Study Areas

Quiet study areas and group-friendly spaces are available to support students, researchers, and community members. Whether you need solo focus time or a collaborative environment, the library provides a productive setting with comfortable seating, good lighting, and helpful staff ready to assist with your learning needs.

Community Events

The library hosts author talks, workshops, tech help sessions, and special events for all ages. These programs foster connection and lifelong learning for the local community and beyond.

Language Resources

Find books and materials in various languages and access online learning tools to explore new cultures or support ESL learners. Holland Park Library is a hub for multilingual and inclusive access.
